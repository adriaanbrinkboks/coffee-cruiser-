Our Story

Two students.
One Land Cruiser.

Coffee Cruiser was born out of a love for coffee and community. Adriaan and Carl started grinding beans in April 2025 and haven't stopped since.

Most weekends we park the Land Cruiser in the neighbourhood and bring handcrafted coffee right to the people. We're not professional baristas; we're two students who love great coffee.
